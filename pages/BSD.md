**Berkeley Software Distribution** was a Unix operating system derivative developed and distributed by the Computer Systems Research Group of the University of California, Berkeley, from 1977 to 1995.

## Modern Varients
- [FreeBSD](?FreeBSD)
- [OpenBSD](?OpenBSD)
- [NetBSD](?NetBSD)
